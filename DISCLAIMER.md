# Important Disclaimer

**OpenAuntie is not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or therapeutic advice.**

OpenAuntie provides general parenting information and tools based on published research and evidence-based parenting programs. It is designed to support parents with practical day-to-day guidance, not to replace qualified professionals.

## What OpenAuntie Is

- A collection of practical tracking tools (behavior points, routines, chores, mood, health, finances)
- A parenting consultant that references evidence-based research to help you think through challenges
- A knowledge base curated from peer-reviewed journals, AAP policy statements, and validated parenting programs

## What OpenAuntie Is NOT

- **Not a therapist or counselor.** OpenAuntie does not diagnose, treat, or provide therapy for mental health conditions in parents or children.
- **Not a medical professional.** Never use OpenAuntie as a substitute for pediatric medical advice. Always consult your child's doctor for health concerns.
- **Not an emergency resource.** If you or someone in your family is in immediate danger, please call 911 or contact the appropriate emergency services. You can also reach the Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline at 1-800-422-4453.
- **Not a replacement for professional evaluation.** If you have concerns about your child's development, behavior, or mental health, please consult a qualified professional.

## When to Seek Professional Help

OpenAuntie will suggest professional consultation when it detects topics outside its scope, including:

- Developmental delays or regression (loss of previously acquired skills)
- Persistent behavioral challenges that don't improve with multiple strategy changes
- Signs of anxiety, depression, or other mental health concerns lasting more than two weeks
- Safety concerns of any kind
- Parental burnout or mental health struggles

**When in doubt, always consult a professional. OpenAuntie errs on the side of recommending professional support.**

## Data Privacy

Your family's stored data (points, routines, health records) is saved as files on your device. OpenAuntie itself never uploads these files. With the **full tracking setup** (Claude Desktop), relevant data snippets may be included in conversation messages sent to the AI provider to give personalized advice. With the **ChatGPT-only setup**, only what you type yourself is sent to OpenAI. For maximum privacy, use nicknames and avoid sharing sensitive medical details.

See [docs/privacy.md](docs/privacy.md) for full details.

## Research Accuracy

The knowledge base is curated from peer-reviewed sources with citations and evidence grading. However, parenting research evolves. If you notice outdated or incorrect information, please open an issue or submit a correction. See [docs/contributing.md](docs/contributing.md).

# Setting Up OpenAuntie with ChatGPT

**No terminal, no GitHub account, no programming.** Just a web browser and a ChatGPT Plus subscription ($20/month).

You get the parenting consultant without the tracking tools.

## What You Get

With ChatGPT, you get:
- The full Auntie parenting consultant personality
- Access to all 15 evidence-based knowledge documents
- Evidence-based advice for any parenting question

What you **don't** get (requires a platform that supports the full toolset, like Claude Desktop):
- Real-time tracking tools (points, routines, chores, etc.)
- Automated weekly summaries
- Family meeting agenda generation

**This gives you about 80% of the value with minimal technical setup.**

## Setup (About 10-15 minutes)

### Step 0: Download OpenAuntie Files

You need the OpenAuntie files on your computer first:

1. Go to [github.com/adgranoff/OpenAuntie](https://github.com/adgranoff/OpenAuntie)
2. Click the green **Code** button
3. Click **Download ZIP**
4. Unzip the downloaded file — you'll find two important folders:
   - `knowledge/` — 15 research documents to upload to your GPT
   - `adapters/` — contains the system prompt file at `adapters/chatgpt_system_prompt.txt`

> **Tip:** You can also view the system prompt directly on GitHub: [chatgpt_system_prompt.txt](https://github.com/adgranoff/OpenAuntie/blob/main/adapters/chatgpt_system_prompt.txt). Click the file, then click the copy button to copy the contents.

Now continue to Step 1.

### Step 1: Create a Custom GPT

1. Go to [chatgpt.com/create](https://chatgpt.com/create) (requires ChatGPT Plus, $20/month)
2. Click **Create** in the top right

### Step 2: Add the System Prompt

You'll find the system prompt file in the downloaded ZIP at `adapters/chatgpt_system_prompt.txt`. Open it with any text editor (Notepad, TextEdit) and copy the contents. Paste them into the **Instructions** field.

You can also copy it directly from GitHub: [chatgpt_system_prompt.txt](https://github.com/adgranoff/OpenAuntie/blob/main/adapters/chatgpt_system_prompt.txt)

### Step 3: Upload Knowledge Files

Click **Knowledge** > **Upload files** and upload ALL files from the `knowledge/` directory:

- developmental_stages.md
- positive_discipline.md
- emotion_coaching.md
- collaborative_problem_solving.md
- behavior_systems.md
- routines_research.md
- homework_and_learning.md
- screen_time.md
- sibling_dynamics.md
- family_communication.md
- financial_literacy.md
- parental_wellbeing.md
- red_flags.md
- evidence_based_scripts.md
- consultant_personality.md

### Step 4: Configure

- **Name**: OpenAuntie
- **Description**: Evidence-based parenting consultant — the trusted auntie every parent deserves
- **Conversation starters** (suggestions):
  - "My kid won't do homework without a fight"
  - "How do I handle sibling fighting?"
  - "What should I expect from a 7-year-old?"
  - "Help me set up a bedtime routine"

### Step 5: Save and Use

Click **Save** (keep it private, share by link, or publish to GPT Store).

Start chatting! Try: "Hi Auntie, my 8-year-old has been having a hard time with homework this week."

## Tips for Best Results

- **Share whatever context you're comfortable with** in your first message — children's ages, a nickname or initial, and what you're working on. Real names are optional.
- **Be specific** about situations: "Last night at bedtime, he refused to brush his teeth and screamed for 20 minutes"
- **Ask follow-up questions**: "What if that doesn't work?" or "Can you give me a specific script?"
- **Ask about research**: "What does the research say about reward charts?"
